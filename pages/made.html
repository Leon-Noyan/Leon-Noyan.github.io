From Farm to Cup: Our Coffee Beans

The Mocha crew takes massive pride in the sourcing of our beans. This is because every good cup starts with a good bean. Therefore, The journey from farm to cup consists of attention to quality control, sustainability and a good relationship with our partners

The Journey

The Mocha Brew makes sure that our coffee beans are selectively sourced from two of the world's most renowned coffee-growing regions. These are none but the high-altitude volcanic soils of Colombia and the lush and fertile lands of Ethiopia.

  • From Colombia: Our beans that originate from Colombia are cultivated in the Andes mountains. These beans get a rich soil, consistent rainfall as well as high elevation. This is the secret to a balanced coffee bean with a smooth, southing taste with a hint of caramel and nutty flavor.
  • From Ethiopia: This is often refered to as the birthplace of coffee. Ethiopia provides us with beans known for their bright and fruity notes.

How we pick the right beans

To ensure the highest quality, our beans are hand-picked by experienced farmers who only select perfectly ripe coffee cherries. By doing this we make sure there is very slim chance for errors in our coffee making process. After the beans have been harvested, the beans get processed using natural methods, this helps refine the flavor.

At The Mocha Brew We believe in strong and ethical relationships. Thus, we only partner with cooperatives that prioritize fair trade practices, by doing this we can be sure that the farmers receive fair compensation for the work that they put in.
